How to Download Capital Gains Statement for SBI Mutual Fund

All SBI Mutual Fund schemes are serviced by CAMS (Computer Age Management Services) as their Registrar and Transfer Agent (RTA). To download your capital gains statement for any SBI MF folio, use the CAMS portal.

Direct Link to Download Capital Gains Statement:
https://www.camsonline.com/Investors/Statements/Capital-Gains-Statement

Step-by-step instructions to download capital gains statement from CAMS:
1. Visit https://www.camsonline.com/Investors/Statements/Capital-Gains-Statement
2. Enter your PAN (Permanent Account Number)
3. Enter your registered email address or mobile number
4. Select the financial year for which you need the capital gains statement
5. Choose statement type: Detailed or Summary
6. Click Submit — the statement will be sent to your registered email

Tax treatment for SBI Equity Funds (Large Cap, Flexicap, Small Cap, Midcap, Focused, Contra):
- Short-term capital gains (STCG): Units held less than 12 months — taxed at 20%
- Long-term capital gains (LTCG): Units held more than 12 months — gains above Rs 1.25 lakh per financial year taxed at 12.5% without indexation

Tax treatment for SBI ELSS Tax Saver Fund:
- Lock-in period: 3 years (statutory)
- Long-term capital gains (LTCG): Gains above Rs 1.25 lakh per financial year taxed at 12.5%
- Tax deduction: Investment up to Rs 1.5 lakh per year eligible for deduction under Section 80C

Tax treatment for SBI Liquid Fund (Debt Fund):
- Short-term capital gains (STCG): Units held less than 36 months — added to income and taxed at slab rate
- Long-term capital gains (LTCG): Units held more than 36 months — taxed at 20% with indexation

Financial year in India runs from April 1 to March 31. Capital gains statements are typically required for income tax filing.
