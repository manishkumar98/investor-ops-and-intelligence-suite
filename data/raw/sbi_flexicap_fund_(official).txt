Fund Data
SBI Flexicap Fund aims to provide investors with opportunities for long-term growth in capital through active management of investments in a diversified basket of equity stocks spanning the entire market capitalization spectrum.
 
The fund invests a minimum of 65% of its assets in equity and equity related instruments across market capitalization.
 
SBI Flexicap Fund can invest up to 35% in debt and money market instruments.
 
The fund follows a bottom-up approach to stock picking and selects companies across sectors/styles.
